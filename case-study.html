Vibrant Brand Identity

CLIENT: [Client Name] / CATEGORY: Brand Identity / YEAR: 2024
OVERVIEW

Project Overview

Vibrant is a modern tech startup focused on bringing color and energy to the digital workspace. The goal was to create a visual identity that felt professional yet approachable, breaking away from the typical "corporate blue" aesthetic.

We developed a comprehensive brand system including logo design, typography, color palette, and digital guidelines. The result is a bold, high-contrast identity that stands out in a crowded market.

The project involved deep strategy sessions to define the brand's core values and target audience, ensuring every visual element served a specific purpose.

ROLE Lead Designer
TIMELINE 8 Weeks
TOOLS Figma, Illustrator
DELIVERABLES Brand System, UI Kit
01
THE CHALLENGE

Breaking the mold.

The primary challenge was balancing the "vibrant" energy with the needs of a B2B audience. We needed to ensure the brand felt exciting without losing credibility. The existing brand was static and lacked a clear voice, making it difficult for the sales team to communicate value effectively.

We had to rethink the entire visual language from the ground up, creating a system that was flexible enough to work across various platforms while maintaining a consistent core.
