Vanguard Studio

CLIENT: Vanguard Arch / CATEGORY: Web Design / YEAR: 2024

Overview

Vanguard Studio is a leading architectural firm that required a digital presence matching their minimalist and bold physical designs. We embarked on a 6-week journey to redefine their brand identity and build a high-performance portfolio website that showcases their projects with cinematic scale.

Lead Designer
6 Weeks
Figma, Webflow, Notion
01

The Challenge

The primary challenge was balancing high-resolution imagery with lightning-fast load times. The firm had a massive archive of 4K photography that needed to be accessible without compromising the user experience. Additionally, the navigation needed to be intuitive yet unconventional to reflect their innovative approach to architecture.

We needed to create a system that allowed the work to speak for itself, removing all unnecessary UI clutter while maintaining a strong brand presence through typography and motion.

02

The Process

[ Process Image ]
01. STRATEGY

Discovery & Audit

We began by auditing their existing assets and identifying the core values of the firm. This phase involved deep-dive workshops to understand their target audience and the emotional impact they wanted their buildings to convey through the screen.

[ Process Image ]
02. DESIGN

Visual Direction

Taking inspiration from brutalist architecture, we developed a high-contrast visual system. We focused on a strict grid layout that could be broken by large, full-bleed imagery to create a sense of space and rhythm.

[ Process Image ]
03. BUILD

Development

The final phase involved building a custom CMS that allowed the Vanguard team to easily upload new projects while maintaining the strict design standards we set. We implemented advanced lazy-loading and image optimization techniques.
