Vanguard Studio Identity.

Client Vanguard Studio
Category Branding & Identity
Year 2024

The Overview

Vanguard Studio is a forward-thinking architectural firm based in Berlin. They needed a visual identity that reflected their minimalist aesthetic and their commitment to sustainable, high-tech construction.

The goal was to create a brand that felt both established and innovative, appealing to high-end developers and private clients alike.

40% Increase in Inquiries
2.5x Brand Recognition
01

The Challenge

The architectural market is saturated with generic "minimalist" brands. Vanguard needed to differentiate itself by leaning into its technical expertise without losing its artistic soul.

02

The Process

[ Strategy ]

We started with a deep dive into the firm's architectural philosophy, identifying "Precision" and "Organic Growth" as the two core pillars of the brand.

[ Design ]

The visual system was built around a custom-drawn monogram and a grid-based typography system that mimics architectural blueprints.

The Results
